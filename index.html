Forty31 Cafe | POS System

Forty31 Cafe’s system was designed with efficiency and user experience in mind. I created a complete UI/UX prototype tailored for the cafe’s staff, enabling them to quickly take customer orders, review daily and weekly sales, and manage their product inventory. The layout is clean, responsive, and easy to navigate, ensuring minimal learning curve for new users. This design prioritizes both speed and accuracy in business operations, helping the cafe run more smoothly during peak hours.

FareMate App

FareMate is a user-friendly mobile application that provides real-time fare estimates for daily commuters and tourists traveling within Batangas. The app was designed to address common pain points like inconsistent fare pricing and a lack of accessible fare information. With FareMate, users can confidently plan their routes, avoid surprise fare hikes, and manage their transportation budget more effectively. Whether you're a local resident or a first-time visitor, FareMate ensures a smoother and more informed commuting experience.

Traffic Guardian

Traffic Guardian is a smart traffic enforcement system that uses AI to detect violations in real time through live camera feeds. It instantly alerts authorities with evidence, logs incidents with detailed data, and provides a dashboard for reviewing and managing cases. With analytics and violator notifications, it helps improve road safety, streamline enforcement, and support smarter urban traffic planning.
